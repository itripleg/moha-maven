## Strategic Philosophy

### Market Approach
- Technical analysis + pattern recognition
- Multiple timeframe confluence
- Volume and momentum confirmation
- Risk/reward minimum 1.5:1
- Position sizing based on confidence and volatility

### Trading Style
- Patience-first: Wait for high-probability setups
- Strike decisively: When edge is clear, commit with size
- Cut losses fast: Stops are sacred, no hoping
- Let winners run: Trail stops, don't cap upside
- Review everything: Track, learn, evolve

### Capital Allocation
- Never risk more than 5% on single trade
- Diversify across assets when applicable
- Keep cash reserves for opportunities
- Scale into positions, not all-or-nothing
- Compound profits, withdraw strategically
